SECTION 1 — RESERVATION & BOOKING (الحجز والتعاقد)
----------------------------------------------------------------

1.1 Reservation Deposit (عربون الحجز)
  - Standard reservation deposit: 20,000 – 50,000 EGP depending on unit price
    • Units under 2,000,000 EGP: 20,000 EGP reservation fee
    • Units 2,000,000 – 5,000,000 EGP: 30,000 EGP reservation fee
    • Units above 5,000,000 EGP: 50,000 EGP reservation fee
  - Reservation fee is deducted from the total downpayment (not extra)
  - Reservation holds the unit for 7 calendar days only
  - Payment method: cash, bank transfer, or certified check payable to company

1.2 Contract Signing (التعاقد)
  - Full contract must be signed within 7 days of reservation
  - Required documents from buyer:
    • National ID (بطاقة الرقم القومي) — original + 2 copies
    • Recent utility bill (to confirm address)
    • For company buyers: commercial registry + authorized signatory docs
  - Downpayment must be paid on day of contract signing
  - Contract is bilingual (Arabic + English) — Arabic version is legally binding

1.3 Downpayment Options (الدفعة الأولى / المقدم)
  - Minimum downpayment varies by payment plan:
    • 5% minimum (available only on select government-supported projects)
    • 10% standard (most projects)
    • 15% – 30% for discounted payment plans (see Section 3)
  - Downpayment cannot be paid in installments — must be paid in one lump sum at signing
  - Cash payment discount applies only if 100% of unit price paid within 30 days of reservation

----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2 — PAYMENT PLAN TERMS (شروط التقسيط)
----------------------------------------------------------------

2.1 Installment Structure (هيكل الأقساط)
  - All installment plans are equal monthly payments (no balloon payments)
  - Installment calculated as: (Total price – Downpayment) ÷ Number of months
  - No interest charged on standard payment plans (0% financing by developer)
  - Plans range from 3 years to 10 years depending on the project
  - Early settlement allowed at any time — 5% discount on remaining balance for early full payment

2.2 Installment Due Dates (مواعيد سداد الأقساط)
  - First installment due 30 days after contract signing date
  - Subsequent installments due on same day of each following month
  - Grace period for late payment: 10 days (no penalty)
  - Late payment penalty after 10-day grace: 0.5% of installment amount per week
  - After 60 days non-payment: company may issue formal notice (إنذار)
  - After 90 days non-payment: company reserves right to cancel contract (subject to cancellation policy)

2.3 Bank Mortgage Option (التمويل البنكي)
  - Company has agreements with the following banks for mortgage financing:
    • Banque Misr (بنك مصر) — up to 15 years, interest rate from 8.5% per annum
    • CIB Egypt (بنك CIB) — up to 20 years, interest rate from 9% per annum
    • Housing and Development Bank / بنك الإسكان والتعمير — subsidized rates for social housing
    • QNB Alahli — up to 15 years
  - Mortgage pre-approval required before signing if using bank finance
  - Company sales team can arrange initial bank consultation — no obligation

2.4 Maintenance Reserve (صندوق الصيانة)
  - One-time maintenance deposit: 8% of unit price, paid at contract signing
  - Maintenance deposit is refundable upon unit resale (at original amount)
  - Annual maintenance fee (رسوم الصيانة): 12–18 EGP/m²/year, due every January
  - Maintenance covers: compound upkeep, pools, green areas, security, building common areas
  - Maintenance does NOT cover in-unit repairs (buyer's responsibility)

SECTION 4 — CANCELLATION POLICY (سياسة الإلغاء)
----------------------------------------------------------------

4.1 Cancellation by Buyer (إلغاء من المشتري)
  - Cancellation within 7 days of reservation: full refund of reservation fee (no deductions)
  - Cancellation after 7 days but before contract signing: 50% of reservation fee is forfeited
  - Cancellation after contract signing but before delivery:
    • Within 30 days of signing: 10% of downpayment forfeited (rest refunded)
    • 30 days to 1 year after signing: 20% of total paid amount forfeited
    • More than 1 year after signing: 30% of total paid amount forfeited
  - All refunds processed within 30–60 working days of cancellation request

4.2 Cancellation by Developer (إلغاء من الشركة)
  - Developer may cancel contract in case of:
    • Non-payment beyond 90 days (after formal notice)
    • Fraudulent documentation provided by buyer
  - Upon developer cancellation for non-payment:
    • Developer refunds all paid amounts minus 20% as administrative fee
    • Refund timeline: up to 90 working days

4.3 Unit Exchange / Transfer (تحويل العقد)
  - Buyer may transfer contract to another party (with company approval)
  - Transfer fee: 1% of remaining unit price (minimum 5,000 EGP)
  - Original buyer remains liable until transfer officially processed
  - Transfer approved within 5 working days upon receipt of full documentation

SECTION 5 — DELIVERY PROCESS (إجراءات الاستلام)
----------------------------------------------------------------

5.1 Delivery Notification (إشعار الاستلام)
  - Company notifies buyer 30 days before delivery via registered mail and SMS/email
  - Buyer must clear all outstanding installments before unit inspection
  - Buyer has 14 days from notification to schedule inspection

5.2 Inspection (المعاينة)
  - Buyer inspects unit with company representative (مشرف الاستلام)
  - Snag list (قائمة الملاحظات) signed by both parties during inspection
  - All snags repaired within 30 days of inspection
  - Second inspection scheduled after snagging completion
  - Buyer must accept delivery within 30 days of snagging completion

5.3 Penalties for Delayed Delivery (عقوبات التأخر في التسليم)
  - If developer delays delivery beyond contracted date by more than 6 months:
    • Buyer may request 0.5% per month reduction on outstanding installments
    • Maximum reduction: 5% of total price
  - Force majeure events (pandemics, natural disasters, government decisions) exempt from penalty
  - Buyer may NOT cancel contract solely due to delay unless delay exceeds 24 months

5.4 Title Deed / Utilities Registration (التسجيل والمرافق)
  - Title deed (سند ملكية) issued within 12–18 months after full payment
  - Water, electricity, and gas connected at delivery (buyer pays connection fees)
  - Electricity connection fee: 3,000–5,000 EGP (varies by project)
  - Water connection: included in maintenance deposit

SECTION 6 — RESALE & INVESTMENT POLICIES (البيع والتحويل)
----------------------------------------------------------------

6.1 Resale Before Delivery (البيع قبل الاستلام)
  - Resale of unit before delivery is allowed with company approval
  - Sub-sale fee: 2% of original unit price
  - Original buyer responsible for all installments until transfer is approved
  - New buyer must qualify through standard documentation process

6.2 Rental of Units
  - Units may be rented after delivery and registration
  - Short-term rental (Airbnb-type) allowed only in designated projects (check per project rules)
  - Compound management must be notified of long-term tenants
  - Tenant must follow compound rules — violations may be reported to owner

6.3 Capital Appreciation
  - Historical average appreciation in New Cairo and 6th of October: 12–18% per annum
  - Off-plan purchase vs. delivery price increase: typically 30–50% over project lifecycle
  - North Coast properties: seasonal, higher rental yield in summer (10–15% annual yield)

SECTION 7 — SALES ETHICS & CLIENT COMMUNICATION (أخلاقيات البيع)
----------------------------------------------------------------

7.1 Salesperson Responsibilities
  - NEVER quote prices not approved in the official price list
  - NEVER promise delivery dates earlier than the official contracted date
  - NEVER offer discounts beyond what is authorized in Sections 3.1 and 3.2
  - ALWAYS disclose the maintenance fee and maintenance deposit before signing
  - ALWAYS confirm payment plan terms in writing — verbal promises are not binding

7.2 Complaints Handling (التعامل مع الشكاوى)
  - Client complaints must be escalated to sales manager within 24 hours
  - Written response to client within 3 working days
  - Escalation path: Sales Rep → Sales Manager → Customer Relations Director

7.3 Anti-Bribery & Gifts Policy
  - Sales representatives may NOT accept gifts valued above 200 EGP from clients
  - Any offered gift above this amount must be reported to management immediately
  - Violation of this policy is grounds for immediate termination

SECTION 8 — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (أسئلة شائعة)
----------------------------------------------------------------

Q: هل ممكن أزود المقدم وأقلل فترة التقسيط؟
A: نعم، الجدول في القسم 3.1 يوضح الخيارات المتاحة. كلما زاد المقدم، كلما قلت فترة التقسيط وزاد الخصم.

Q: Can a foreigner (non-Egyptian) buy?
A: Yes. Foreign nationals may purchase with valid passport. Additional paperwork required (residence permit or notarized POA). Resale restricted — cannot resell within 5 years without government approval for foreign buyers.

Q: هل فيه خصم للموظفين الحكوميين؟
A: لا يوجد خصم خاص للموظفين الحكوميين في مشاريعنا الحالية. الخصومات متاحة للجميع وفقاً لخطط الدفع المحددة.

Q: Can I pay with cryptocurrency?
A: No. Company accepts only EGP cash, bank transfer, or certified checks. No foreign currency or cryptocurrency accepted.

Q: هل المشاريع مسجلة رسمياً في الشهر العقاري؟
A: نعم، جميع مشاريعنا مرخصة من وزارة الإسكان وهيئة المجتمعات العمرانية. سند الملكية يُصدر بعد السداد الكامل خلال 12–18 شهراً.

Q: What happens if I lose my job and can't pay installments?
A: We offer a one-time installment deferral for up to 3 months (no penalty) upon written request with documentation. Extensions beyond 3 months are subject to management approval and may require restructuring the payment plan.
